Community

Situated in Salasala area in Kinondoni Municipal, Tegeta district, in the commercial city of Dar es Salaam, the school serves the education needs of students all over The United Republic of Tanzania and especially International and Foreign students. The school was established in 2013 and has a very diverse international community of students and staff. School

We are offering Cambridge International Education System, English as a first language with qualified teachers from various countries and also from Tanzania. Teachers are carefully selected and employed as our main target is to give a good, suitable and quality education to our students in our school. The school is involved in various community based projects as an integral part to grow up responsible young generation who are concerned with the problems facing the society. We envision our graduates to be part of the solution in the society and eventually become productive and good citizens.
